Rebecca D'Agostino

Conditional Design Project:
Instructions:
Each player starts on a 18x24 inch piece of paper with different colored markers.
1. Each player will have 5 seconds to draw a spiral (of any size) anywhere on the page.
2. The next player’s spiral must connect to either the center or end of the last player’s spiral (cannot be branched from any random curve/side).
3. Players have the option to overlap spirals or branch theirs onto a clean space on the page.
4. All players will take turns drawing connecting spirals until the page is full.
