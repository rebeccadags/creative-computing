Body of Knowledge

Hiking with Aubrey

by Rebecca D'Agostino

Why do you hike?
I hike because my family has always been the outdoorsy type. We were the kind of family that would rather go camping out west for a week than to Disney World. Being outside, being with the ones I love, and exploring life's beautiful creations is why I hike.
What do you normally wear while hiking?
While hiking I normally wear leggings, a t-shirt of some shorts (preferable my yellow life is good t-shirt), bean boots with high socks (I don't want to get any blisters) and my favorite baseball hat at the moment which ever that may be.

What season is the best for hiking?
Definitly fall! This is because it's not too hot to where I'm drenched in sweat but not too cold to where I can't feel my fingers. Its a happy medium!
What do you do while hiking other than walk?
Other than walk, I like to sometimes play music from my phone, take photos of beautiful landscapes, or if my dog comes along, play catch with him.
Is it enjoyable just because of the outdoors? Or is there another element that makes it worth it?
Other than the outdoors, I enjoy hiking because it gives me a chance to remove myself from the hassle and fuss of everyday life. It's a time where I can disconnect and truly live life in the moment. Along with this, it reminds me of who I am, where I come from, what I've been through and how lucky I am to be given such a life.
